## Transportation and Parking

### Campus Parking

There are paid parking garages and surface lot parking available on campus with a visitor daily permit.

* [Purdue Visitor Parking website](https://www.purdue.edu/operations/parking/home/permits/visitors/)
* [West Lafayette Campus Parking Map](https://purdueuniversity.maps.arcgis.com/apps/instant/basic/index.html?appid=47529701e46e48a08289fc46cb6de802)

#### Parking Garages

**Grant Street Parking Garage**

* Located at 120 North Grant Street, West Lafayette, IN 47906 across from the Purdue Memorial Union
* Rates is $20 per day ($5 for first hour, $2 per additional hour)

  * **NOTE:** The daily visitor parking permit will NOT work for this gated parking garage
* .02 miles from conference location (estimated 4 minute walk)



#### Visitor Daily Permit

Motorists may purchase an $8 visitor daily permit and park in any “A”, “B” or “C” space (as applicable), **excluding** the Grant Street and Harrison Street garages (virtual passes do not work for these gated garages).

Daily visitor parking permits must be purchased online through the [Purdue Parking Portal](https://purdue.t2hosted.com/Account/Portal). Review the [campus parking map](https://purdueuniversity.maps.arcgis.com/apps/instant/basic/index.html?appid=47529701e46e48a08289fc46cb6de802) for permit locations. Overnight parking is NOT included with visitor permits.

**Closest non-gated garages to conference location:**

* Northwestern Avenue Garage

  * 504 Northwestern Ave, West Lafayette, IN 47906
  * .04 miles from conference location (under 10 minute walk)
* Wood Street Garage

  * 120 S Grant St, West Lafayette, IN 47906
  * .02 miles from conference location (5 minute walk)
  * **NOTE:** This garage is under construction for the summer semester, please check [campus parking map](https://purdueuniversity.maps.arcgis.com/apps/instant/basic/index.html?appid=47529701e46e48a08289fc46cb6de802) (WL construction) before deciding to park here



## Traveling Information

### Driving

Purdue University is located in West Lafayette, Indiana which is about 65 miles north of Indianapolis and 125 miles south of Chicago off of I-65. Taking I-65 north or south, you can get to campus by taking exit 172 and following State Road 26 (right coming from the north, left coming from the south) about 5 miles through Lafayette and into West Lafayette where it will become State Street. Continue up State Street hill and move into the right lane to get to the campus.

### Flying

#### Local

[Purdue University Airport](https://www.purdue.edu/airport/)

**Purdue University Airport** (code: LAF) is located on Purdue West Lafayette’s campus and offers commercial flights connecting to O’Hare International Airport in Chicago. Purdue University has partnered with SkyWest for commercial air service through United Express, a regional network operated by United Airlines.

* 1515 Aviation Drive, West Lafayette, IN 47907
* Open daily: 5 a.m. to 12 a.m.
* [United Express FAQs](https://www.purdue.edu/airport/frequently-asked-questions/)

#### Regional

[Indianapolis International Airport](https://www.ind.com/)

**Indianapolis International Airport** (code: IND) is approximately 65 miles south of Purdue and is serviced by most major airlines and their partner airlines. The [Lafayette Limo](https://www.lafayettelimo.com/?_ga=2.41863142.534276663.1784558235-1090116273.17805095530) (or [Reindeer Shuttle](https://www.reindeershuttle.com/)) provides daily ground shuttle service both ways between the Indianapolis International Airport and Purdue University. Shuttle service between the Purdue University Airport and some local hotels is available. Please call the local hotel number or check their website for more information.

**Driving directions from Indianapolis**

* Take I-65 North (toward Chicago) to Lafayette and exit on State Road IN 26 (exit 172)
* Turn left at the light and follow IN 26 approximately 5 miles through Lafayette and into West Lafayette where it will become State Street
* The Hilton Garden Inn and Hampton Inn are located at the bottom of State Street
* Continue up State Street hill and move into the right lane to get to the campus
* In less than a mile, the Grant Street parking garage will be located on your right, just before you hit the edge of campus

[Chicago - O’Hare](https://www.flychicago.com/ohare/home/pages/default.aspx?_ga=2.45624416.534276663.1784558235-1090116273.1780509553)

**Chicago O'Hare International Airport** (code: ORD) is located approximately 140 miles north of Greater Lafayette and is approximately a 3 hour drive from the airport to Purdue. The [Lafayette Limo](https://www.lafayettelimo.com/?_ga=2.41863142.534276663.1784558235-1090116273.17805095530) (or [Reindeer Shuttle](https://www.reindeershuttle.com/)) provides daily ground shuttle service both ways between the Purdue University Airport and Chicago's O'Hare Airport. Shuttle service between the Purdue University Airport and some local hotels is available. Please call the local hotel number or check their website for more information.

**Driving directions from Chicago**

* Take I-65 South to State Road 26 (exit 172)
* Turn right onto State Road 26 West and follow it approximately 5 miles through Lafayette and into West Lafayette, where it will become State Street
* The Hilton Garden Inn and Hampton Inn are located at the bottom of State Street
* Continue up State Street hill and move into the right lane to get to the campus
* In less than a mile, the Grant Street parking garage will be located on your right, just before you hit the edge of campus
